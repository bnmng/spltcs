From North of Suffolk

  • Take I64 or I264 to US13/58/460 to Suffolk.
  • Avoid the business routes and avoid exits for Downtown Suffolk. Stay on regular US13/58/460.
  • After about 13 miles, take the right exit to stay on US13 South towards Ahoskie and Edenton.
  • After about 3 miles, exit right again to stay on US13 South.
  • Drive for about one mile.
  • Turn right into the driveway immediately after the red, arch-roofed building.

From South of Suffolk

  • Take US13 North to Suffolk.
  • About 13 miles North of the Virginia/North Carolina border, slow down as you pass Airport Rd and Old Somerton Road
  • Make the first left turn after Colony Tire to your left, before the red, arch-roofed building.
